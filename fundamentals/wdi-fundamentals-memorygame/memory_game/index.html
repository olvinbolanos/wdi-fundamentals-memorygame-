Instructions

Concentration, also know as Match Match, Memory, Pelmanism, Shinkei-suijaku, Pexeso or simpoly Pairsi, is a card game in which all of the cards are laid face down on a surface and two cards are flipped face up over each turn. The object of the game is to turn over pairs of matching cards.

What's fun about it? Why should people play?
